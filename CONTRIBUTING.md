🤝 CONTRIBUTING GUIDE

Muna maraba da gudunmawa daga duk masu sha'awar taimakawa aikin Hisbah Board Sokoto Dashboard. Ga yadda za ka iya bada gudunmawa:

📦 Yadda Ake Gudunmawa

1. Fork repo ɗin

   `bash

   `bash
